DSmini help
when first run, DSmini will setup 4 channels to select from. An alert will display to inform you.
use the menu on the upper right to do something. Use set video to choose a channel and select a video.
Clicking on a profile image from the comments will take you to the replies for that comment.
Clicking on a profile image from the comments will TDS transport you to that comment in the Youtube app.
CLOSE the youtube app before you TDS transport. I it isnt closed first, it wont load a different comment.
